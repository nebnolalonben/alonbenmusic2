oku studio

In February 2023, I opened my new studio space. It is located in the beautiful countryside, right outside Basel, surrounded by fields and trees, and is part of a complete house with sleeping and cooking facilities, as well as direct access to the garden. At this point, the studio has been put to the test heavily and is used weekly for recording, producing, and mixing. My idea is to work together in a creative space filled with good vibes, using vintage instruments and equipment that enable ideas to flow while having the sound sources ready to record at all times to maintain inspiration and capture a performance in the best-suited recording quality on either Tape or digital. 

To get in touch, book sessions or just to say hi send me a message via the contact form :) 
 Alon Ben Music
